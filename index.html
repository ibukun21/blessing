About Us

Clane Company Nigeria Ltd. is a Lagos based company leveraging the power of technology to provide people

with easy-to-use financial products and services . We bring together developers, investors and experts in

finance and digital technology to deliver innovative solutions that transform today’s financial landscape.

Our Culture

At Clane, we value people and relationships over revenues, and this shows in our company’s culture. We’ve worked hard
to build a positive environment that spans across and throughout our entire organization. We see the immense benefits
technology has to offer to people throughout the world but we are also aware of the challenges of its limited accessibility.
We have built our company culture around the notion that every last person in Nigeria should have access to digital
technology and the ability to use it to foster a more organized and efficient lifestyle.
